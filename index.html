Homepage

The International Society for Universal Dialogue was founded in Warsaw, Poland in 1989. Its main purpose is to pursue, by means of scholarly work, the gradual realization of a decent, peaceful, and just world order. Towards this goal, ISUD hosts biannual international conferences to facilitate an ethos of universal dialogue.
